Teens

The term "teens" typically refers to individuals who are in their teenage years, which generally span the ages of 13 to 19. This period of life is characterized by significant physical, emotional, and social development. Teenagers are often in the process of transitioning from childhood to adulthood, and they experience a variety of changes and challenges during this time. It's a crucial stage in personal identity formation, education, and social relationships. The term "teens" is commonly used to collectively refer to this age group.

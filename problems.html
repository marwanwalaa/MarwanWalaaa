Problems !

Generation Z faces a range of challenges shaped by the digital age, economic uncertainties, environmental concerns, and social and political upheavals. Growing up in a world characterized by constant connectivity and social media, they encounter mental health issues, cyberbullying, and information overload. Economic challenges, coupled with disruptions in education and a competitive job market, contribute to uncertainties about the future. Generation Z is also actively engaged in addressing issues of diversity and inclusion, climate change, and social justice, reflecting a strong sense of responsibility and activism. Despite these challenges, this generation exhibits resilience, adaptability, and a commitment to positive change, emphasizing the need for holistic solutions that address their unique circumstances.
